Jeddah Albalad

Jeddah Albalad is the historical heart of Jeddah, showcasing the Kingdom's rich heritage. Its coral houses echo authentic Hijazi architecture. Traditional markets and ancient cafes line its alleys. Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage site, it's a must-visit cultural treasure in Saudi Arabia.
